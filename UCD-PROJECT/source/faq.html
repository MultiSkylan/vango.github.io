Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need a special licence to drive a Campervan in Ireland?
    All our vehicles can be driven on a standard full car drivers license (B License). International drivers will require an International driver’s license if the license issued by their country of residence is not in English.
  • How old do I need to be drive a Rent A Camper vehicle?
    Drivers must be 25 years of age or over. If you are over the age of 70 years, please contact us prior to booking a medical certificate is required.
  • Is there a minimum rental duration?
    All Rent A Camper have minimum rental period of 4 days.
